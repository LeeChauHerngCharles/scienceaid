Moment of force

The turning effect of a force is called its moment or torque. The turning effect of a force a pivot depends on the size of the force and the perpendicular distance from the line of the force to the pivot.

Definition of Moment

Moment of force = Force x Perpendicular distance from the line of action of the force to the pivot.

SI unit of moment of force is newton metre.

Principle of Moments

When an object is in equilibrium, the sum of clockwise moments about any point is equal to the sum of anticlockwise moments about the same point.
