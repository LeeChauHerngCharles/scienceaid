Compunds

A compound is a pure substance containing two or more elements that are chemically combined in a fixed ratio. Compounds maybe be made up of molecules, atoms or ions. Compounds have different properties from the elements that form it.

Decomposition

A chemical process that breaks down the compound into its elements is called decomposition. Thermal decompsition involes heating to the compound causing it to decompose. Electrolysis is the process of using electricity to break down a compound.
