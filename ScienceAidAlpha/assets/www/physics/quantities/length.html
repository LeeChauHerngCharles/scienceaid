Measurement of Length

The SI Unit for length is the metre. It is necessary to use the appropriate instruments and methods to measure different types of length.

Precision of an instrument

The smallest unit an instrument can measure is known as its precision.

The calipers

Calipers are used for measuring the diameters of cylinders or curcular objects.

The vernier calipers

The vernier calipers consist of a main scale and a sliding vernier scale. It is a useful tool that is used to measure both the internal and external diameters of an object. The precision of the vernier calipers is 0.01 cm.

The micrometer screw gauge

The micrometer screw gauge is used to measure lengths to a precision of 0.01 mm or 10 micrometers. It can measure the external diameter of wires and ball bearings.
