What is the difference between mass and weight?

Mass

The mass of an object is a measure of the amount of substance in the object. It is measured using a beam balance.

Weight

The weight of an object is the force of gravity acting upon that object. It is measured using a spring balance.

W = mg

Inertia

The inertia of an object is the reluctance of the object to change either its rest, or if it is moving, its motion in a straight line.
