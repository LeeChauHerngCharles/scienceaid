The Need for Food

All living organisms require to stay alive.

Photosynthesis

Photosynthesis is the process by which green plants use light energy from the Sun to convert raw materials from their surroundings into food such as carbohydrates, thus storing chemical energy.

Role of food to make new protoplasm

The body of a living organism undergoes wear and tear and is able to grow, reproduce and repair its worn out parts. To carry out these processes, organisms must make new protoplasm from certain substances found in food.

Role of food in health

Certain substances in food help to keep your body healthy. For instance, a substance called vitamin C, found in limes, prevents your gums from bleeding and your teeth from falling out.
