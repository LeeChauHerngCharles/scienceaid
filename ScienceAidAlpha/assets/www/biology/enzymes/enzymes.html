Enzymes are Biological Catalysts

Enzymes are biological catalysts made of protein. They alter the rate of chemcical reactions without themselves being chemically changed at the end of the reactions.

Catalyst

A catalyst is a substance which can alter or speed up a chemical reaction, without itself being chemically changed at the end of the reaction.

Activation energy

The energy needed to start a chemical reaction is called activation energy. Enzymes lower activation energy needed to digest the food substances.
